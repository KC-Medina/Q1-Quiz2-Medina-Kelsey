Ratatouille

Remy, a rat, desires to be a brilliant chef in spite of his family's disapproval, but there's the obvious issue of being a rat in a profession that is obviously rodent-phobic.
Remy finds himself in the perfect location beneath a restaurant made famous by his culinary hero, Auguste Gusteau, until fate drops him into Paris' sewers.
